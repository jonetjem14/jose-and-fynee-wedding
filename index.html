Frequently Asked Questions

What time should I arrive? ▼

Guests are invited to arrive at 3:00 PM for the reception. The ceremony will begin at 4:30 PM. We recommend arriving a bit earlier to enjoy welcome drinks and mingle with other guests.

Can I bring a plus one? ▼

Plus-ones are invited! Please make sure to include them in your RSVP count. If your invitation says "and Guest," feel free to bring someone special.

What should I wear? ▼

Please refer to the dress code mentioned in the "What to Expect" section. Formal attire for the ceremony and smart casual for the reception. Bring a light jacket as Baguio can get cool in the evening.

Is there parking available? ▼

Yes! Hotel Supreme has ample parking available for all guests. Simply drive to the hotel and our valet service will assist you.

Do you have dietary restrictions? ▼

Absolutely! Please mention any dietary restrictions when you RSVP. We've arranged for vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other special meal options.

When is the RSVP deadline? ▼

Please RSVP by August 15, 2026. This helps us finalize headcounts and seating arrangements. Late RSVPs may not be accommodated.
