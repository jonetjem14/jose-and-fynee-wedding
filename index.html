Our Story

How We Met

+

Some love stories begin with grand plans. Ours began with two friends who simply believed we should meet.

Through our dear friends, Kael and Ritch, fate quietly introduced us to one another. We met for the very first time at Metro Central Mall in Sta. Cruz, Laguna. What was supposed to be a double date quickly turned into something much more special when our friends "unexpectedly" left us to spend the evening together.

Our first stop was La Esperanza Cake House & Coffee Shop, where our very first conversation over coffee began. It wasn't exactly a perfect start. Jose realized he had forgotten to bring cash and only had online banking. As he hurried away to withdraw money, Fynee wondered if she had just been left behind. But true to his word, he came back, and together we laughed about what would become one of our favorite memories.

Who made the first move? How did it happen?

+

Jose did. After our very first date, he couldn't stop thinking about Fynee. The very next morning, he surprised her at her office with a bouquet of beautiful white flowers. With all of her coworkers happily teasing them, it was a moment neither of them would ever forget.

From that day on, one date turned into many, weekly meetups became something they both looked forward to, and before they knew it, friendship had blossomed into a love they knew was worth holding on to forever.

What’s your favorite thing to do together?

+

Our favorite thing is simply being together. Whether we're watching movies, going for walks or jogs, enjoying date nights, traveling, or discovering beautiful places, every moment becomes special because we're sharing it with each other.

Above all, the greatest joy we share is growing in our faith and serving the Lord Jesus Christ together. From joining church activities to lifting our voices in worship every Sunday, we pray that God will always remain at the center of our relationship and the foundation of our marriage. ❤️

Recommended Neighborhoods

If you'd like to stay together as a group, have a more independent stay or turn the trip into a longer getaway, explore the best neighborhoods near our venue.

⏱️ Best for Short Stays

Burnham Park / Session Road

📍 2.3 km from venue • 8 min drive

Shortest commute to Hotel Supreme Convention Plaza (central roads and taxis available).

Very walkable: parks, cafes, restaurants and the Public Market within easy walking distance.

Good public transport and taxi availability for guests without cars.

💑 Best for Couples

Camp John Hay

📍 3.6 km from venue • 11 min drive

Tourist-friendly: scenic walking trails and historic sites provide a relaxed stay.

Many mid-size hotels, chalets and condos suitable for couples and families.

Short taxi or shuttle ride to the venue; calmer evenings than downtown.

🏡 Best for Families

Mines View / Wright Park

📍 1.3 km from venue • 20 min walk

Close to major scenic attractions (Mines View Park, Wright Park) — good for families and first-time visitors.

Mix of guesthouses and small hotels with easy access to viewpoint trails.

Short drive (10–20 minutes) to the venue while offering quieter nights than downtown.

📅 Best for Long Stays

Teachers Camp / Loakan Area

📍 1.7 km from venue • 4 min drive

More spacious properties and quieter evenings — suitable for longer stays and groups.

Good road access to the venue while offering greater privacy and on-site parking.

Some properties are set in greener, less touristy parts of the city.

🚗 Getting There

If you're driving: Simply enter "Hotel Supreme Baguio" in Google Maps or Waze. The hotel is located along Magsaysay Avenue, one of Baguio City's main roads, and is easy to find.

If you're taking public transportation:

  • From Victory Liner or Genesis Bus Terminal: The venue is about 10–15 minutes away by taxi (depending on traffic).
  • From SM City Baguio or Session Road: It's approximately 10–15 minutes by taxi or ride-hailing service.

Helpful Tip: Baguio traffic can be heavier on weekends and holidays, so we kindly recommend leaving a little earlier to ensure you arrive on time.
